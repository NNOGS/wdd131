Setup Tutorial

This page guides you through setting up your Naija HomeTech smart devices fast and easy.

Setting Up a Smart Bulb

  • Screw the bulb into your lamp or ceiling holder.
  • Turn on the light switch.
  • Install the companion mobile app.
  • Ensure your phone is connected to 2.4GHz Wi-Fi.
  • Open the app and choose “Add Device”.
  • Select “Smart Bulb” and follow the on-screen instructions.

Setting Up a Smart Plug

  • Plug the smart plug into your wall socket.
  • Connect your appliance (fan, kettle, etc.).
  • Open the app → tap “Add Device”.
  • Select “Smart Plug”.
  • Rename the plug for easy identification.
  • Test switching it ON/OFF from the app.

Setting Up a Smart Remote

  • Insert batteries into the remote.
  • Open the app and choose “Add Device”.
  • Select “IR Remote”.
  • Pair it with your Wi-Fi.
  • Add your TV, AC, decoder, or any IR device.

Troubleshooting

  • Not connecting? Reset the device and retry.
  • Bulb blinking? It’s in pairing mode.
  • Device offline? Ensure Wi-Fi is 2.4GHz.
  • Plug not responding? Remove and reinsert it.

FAQ

  • Do these devices work without internet? Some functions work locally, but remote control needs internet.
  • Can I control them outside Nigeria? Yes, as long as the device is online and your app has internet.
  • Can multiple users control a device? Yes, just share access in the app.

Naija HomeTech – Website Planning Document

A practical guide to affordable smart home gadgets in Nigeria.

Site Name

Naija HomeTech

This name reflects a Nigerian-focused resource for smart home technology. It is simple, memorable, and clearly communicates the purpose of the site.

Optional domain: naijahometech.com (availability not required)

Site Purpose

The purpose of Naija HomeTech is to explain and recommend affordable smart home devices available in Nigeria. The site provides:

  • Beginner-friendly explanations of smart home technology.
  • Device listings with features, price estimates, and usage ideas.
  • Step-by-step setup tutorials and troubleshooting tips.
  • Guides on improving Wi-Fi coverage and power efficiency at home.

Scenarios

  • “Which affordable smart home gadgets work well with Nigeria’s power conditions?”
  • “How do I set up a smart bulb or camera without technical experience?”
  • “What devices can help me automate lighting and security on a budget?”

Color Scheme

The site will use the following color palette:

  • Primary – #1a73e8: headings, buttons, highlights
  • Secondary – #0a2a43: header, footer backgrounds
  • Light – #f5f7fa: page background
  • Accent – #ffd54f: important emphasis elements
  • Text – #333: body text

(These colors are applied directly in this site plan.)

Typography

Font Chosen: Inter (Google Fonts)

  • Headings: Inter – bold weights
  • Body text: Inter – regular weight
  • Navigation & buttons: Inter – semibold
